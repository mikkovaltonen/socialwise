# Valmet Finland Supplier and Spend Data 2023

## Overview

This document contains business partner spending, supplier information, and training supplier data for Valmet Finland operations in 2023.

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Total Annual Spend** | €38,854,378.43 |
| **Total Suppliers** | 473 |
| **Total Invoices Processed** | 7,291 |
| **Average Payment Time** | Varies by supplier (15-60 days typical) |

## Spend Distribution by Category

### Main Categories

1. **IPPS - Professional Services**: 349 suppliers
2. **IPPE - Personnel**: 122 suppliers

## Supplier Compliance Metrics

### E-Invoicing Adoption
- Suppliers are tracked for e-invoicing capability
- Electronic invoicing improves processing efficiency

### PO Coverage
- Purchase Order coverage is monitored for spend control
- Higher PO coverage indicates better procurement compliance

### SSC Policy Compliance
- Suppliers are tracked for Shared Service Center (SSC) policy signature
- Most major suppliers have signed SSC policies

## Payment Terms

Standard payment terms used:
- **STOP_N30**: Net 30 days
- **STOP_N45**: Net 45 days  
- **STOP_N60**: Net 60 days

## Data Structure

The complete dataset includes:
1. **Spend Export**: Detailed spending data by supplier
2. **Supplier Data with FI Spend**: Comprehensive supplier information
3. **FI Data Cleaned**: Processed Finland-specific data
